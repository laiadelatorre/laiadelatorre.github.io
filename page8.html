Beyond the Score. Mozart & Chopin
PIANO SOLO ALBUM (2025)

Repertoire: W.A.Mozart, F.Chopin and L.De La Torre
Mixing and mastering: Tower Productions

In this piano solo album, Laia De La Torre reinterprets works by Mozart and Chopin, followed by an original composition. By subtly re-imagining the turns and ornamentation in the melodies - much as composers themselves might have done - the album offers a fresh perspective on these timeless pieces. Moreover, Laia's own composition blends stylistic elements of both Mozart and Chopin, creating a bridge between their musical worlds. More than just a performance, this album is a conversation with the music, exploring its depth and hidden subtleties. Balancing tradition with artistic freedom, the album invites listeners to rediscover these masterpieces through a lens that connects interpretation and improvisation.
