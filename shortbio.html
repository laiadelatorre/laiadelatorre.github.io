About Laia

Laia is a classical pianist, originally from a town near Barcelona. As a true believer of the transforming power that music has over society, she does not only like to explore music by performing it but also through the world of musical composition and improvisation.

Laia, THE PIANIST

Laia began her piano studies at a very young age with her mom. Ultimately, she got graduated at Conservatori Superior de Música del Liceu of Barcelona under the teaching of Àlex Alguacil in piano and Emili Brugalla in chamber music.

Laia has performed numerous times at social and cultural venues across Catalonia and internationally, and have participated in many masterclasses by world-renowned pianists. Recently, she has debuted with the Orquestra do Algarve, playing Chopin's Piano Concerto in E minor, under the baton of Joshua dos Santos.  

She likes to share some of her concerts and the repertoire she plays on her Youtube channel.

Laia, THE COMPOSER

Musical composition (and also film scoring) was of Laia's interest from a very young age. She has learnt the craft of this field mostly in a self-taught manner, but loves to share her own compositions on her Youtube channel. Nowadays, she is being tutored by the composer Mike Ladouceur. 

Some of her compositions has been awarded and premiered internationally. Recently, she has been commissioned to compose the music for a new ballet play. 

Additionally, Laia also has delved into the world of musical improvisation. She believes that improvisation is key for the understanding of music. For this reason, she loves to improvise music on the piano. This fact has led Laia to the world of dance accompaniment so that she is currently working in the High-Performance Centre of Dance in Terrassa (PAR) and in IEA Oriol Martorell in Barcelona.
