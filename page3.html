About Laia

Laia is a classical pianist born in 1999, originally from a town near Barcelona. As a true believer of the transforming power that music has over society, she does not only like to explore music by performing it but also through the world of musical composition and improvisation.
Laia, the pianist

Laia began her piano studies at a very young age with her mom. Ultimately, she got graduated at Conservatori Superior de Música del Liceu of Barcelona under the teaching of Àlex Alguacil. 
Laia has performed numerous times at social and cultural venues across Catalonia and have participated in many masterclasses by world-renowned pianists. 

Laia, the chamber music pianist

In 2018, during their bachelor's degree, two colleagues and herself formed a classical piano trio called Trio Aqua, under the teaching of Emili Brugalla. Since then, they have performed in several stages of Catalonia and Italy and have participated in masterclasses by renowned musicians. 

Laia, the composer

Musical composition (and also film scoring) was of Laia's interest from a very young age. She has learnt the craft of this field mostly in a self-taught manner, but loves to share her own compositions on her Youtube channel. 

Laia, the improviser

Laia believes that musical improvisation is key for the understanding of music. For this reason, she loves to improvise music on the piano. 

Also, she is currently working as a ballet accompanist in the High-Performance Centre of Dance in Terrassa (PAR).
